About Us

Wai Genetics is a blue bio-technology company which monitors the microbiology in water. Our insights, quickly and accurately map the status of the bacterial balance from a water sample.

With use of high precision analysis tools, with most advanced technology and machine learning we provide information for smarter decision-making in order to reduce risk and increase profitability in biological production.
